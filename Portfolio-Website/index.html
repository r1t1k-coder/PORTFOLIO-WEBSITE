About Me
Designing Is My Passion

Passion is what motivates me to achieve incredible things. At the age of 14, I found my passion for design, which was still unfamiliar to my friends around that age. I started learning design because I was inspired by the innovative power of drawing online. Having learned techniques such as illustrations, vectors or color scheme on design apps and websites, design became my passion because it introduced me to a world where I was able to make personal works of art and express my thoughts and ideas without limits. Design has honed my artistic sensibility as I really enjoy conveying the imaginative world inside me, and I was pleased to see my work praised by friends and commenters online.
